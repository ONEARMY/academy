Intro

Title of your project: Is it indicative of the goal? Is it easy to understand? Remember, keep it short.

Select tags (max 4): Select accurate tags, then your project will be found.

How long does it take?: Helps users plan their time.

Difficulty level: Objective, we know! Try to select from the type of user that will be completing it. Creating a basic mould from someone that has no experience will probably find it quite hard. But for a mould maker, easy.

Short description: keep it to 300 characters please! Upload files: do you have CAD drawings, testing sheets, or resources that will help? Zip them in a folder and upload them here. Aim to keep it under mb.

Cover photo: upload a landscape photo that shows your product or process clearly. We are visual creatures, good imagery will communicate quicker than text will. Keep it to 1280x960px.
